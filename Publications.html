I am a fifth year PhD student in Computer Science at North Carolina State University.
My advisor is Prof. Xiaohui (Helen) Gu. I am a member of DANCE (Distributed system research on Autonomy, resilieNce, Collaboration, and Energy) Group.
My general research interests are in the areas of systems, with a current focus on applying static code analysis and machine learning techniques on cloud computing and system reliability. I am also interested in blockchain and cryptography technologies.
Before joining NC State, I received my Master degree in 2014 and Bachelor degree in 2011 from Nanjing University of Posts and Telecommunications, China.
